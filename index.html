<h2><strong>about</strong></h2>

<hr />

<p>Premkumar Mudaliar is Vice President, Business Technology Services at United Overseas Bank (UOB), Singapore; project management, business analysis,&nbsp;development and delivery of applications in Cash Management &amp; Trade space.</p>

<p>Prem received Bachelor of Commerce degree with specialization in Banking &amp; Finance and Cost &amp; Works Accounting from University of Pune, India in 1999 and Systems Management program at NIIT, Pune with professional practice as Faculty &amp; Technical Lead at NIIT, Aurangabad in 2000. He was certified as Java Programmer by Sun Microsystems and received diploma in Export Management in 2000.</p>

<p>Prem worked as Analyst Programmer from 2001 - 02 in India and moved to Singapore where he continued in similar role till 2003. He worked as Senior Analyst Programmer till 2004 and as Consultant till 2006 before beaming Project Leader. He worked as Senior Project Manager till 2012 before moving to work for United Overseas Bank.</p>
